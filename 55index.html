About Caspian Teb

Caspian Teb Nedaye Salamat Pars is a specialized manufacturer of disposable sterile personal protective equipment (PPE) serving hospitals, clinics, and industrial clients. We produce sterile surgical gowns, disposable surgical packs, shoe covers, caps, and other single‑use protective items designed to reduce cross‑contamination and support safe clinical workflows. Our production lines combine modern non‑woven materials with validated sterilization processes to deliver consistent, reliable products for critical environments.
